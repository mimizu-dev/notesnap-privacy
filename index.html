NoteSnap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

No data collection

NoteSnap does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All notes are saved locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local.

Obsidian sync

When you choose to sync, notes are sent directly from your browser to your own Obsidian vault running at 127.0.0.1 (your own computer). No data passes through any third-party server.

Microphone

Microphone access is used solely for on-device speech recognition via the browser's built-in Web Speech API. Audio is never recorded, stored, or uploaded anywhere.

No analytics or telemetry

NoteSnap contains no analytics, tracking scripts, or telemetry of any kind.
